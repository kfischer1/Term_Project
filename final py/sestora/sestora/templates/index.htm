Sestora

Searching for a place to store your belongings is not always an easy task. Physical brick and mortar storage facilities can be expensive, hard to get to, or poorly managed. Another option could be to leave your stuff with a friend for an extended period of time - but even that can be hard to coordinate or arrange. Wouldn't it be great if you could leave your stuff with a local member of your community?

Sestora is a simple, easy to use platform where individuals can rent their unused space. Think of it as an AirBnB for self storage.
